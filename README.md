Customer support stands out as a genuine strength. The team responds quickly—often within minutes—and they actually solve problems rather than just deflecting them. This seems especially true during their promotional periods when they clearly staff up to handle the increased volume.

Price-to-performance ratio is where TotHost really shines. Multiple reviewers specifically mention being surprised by what they get for the money, especially during promotional periods. One user noted getting VPS performance comparable to providers charging 2-3x the price.

**Areas for Consideration:**

Network connectivity occasionally shows its geographical limitations. A few users report that connections can be unstable during Vietnam's fiber infrastructure issues, though this seems more about regional internet stability than TotHost's own infrastructure.

The Vietnam location, while a selling point for Asian market targeting, means higher latency for North American or European visitors. If your audience is primarily Western, you'll want to factor this into your decision.

Some users wish for more data center location options. While the Hanoi locations work well for the target market, expansion to other Asian cities would increase flexibility.
